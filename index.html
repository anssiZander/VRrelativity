Relativistic observer VR demo

β = v/c 0.80
Desktop: click the scene, then use WASD to move, Q/E up/down, mouse to look, Shift to boost, Esc to release mouse.
VR: press Enter VR, then turn your controller or hand palm up to open the menu. Left thumbstick = move/strafe in headset look direction, right thumbstick = turn + vertical fly.
